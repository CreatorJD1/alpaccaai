AI keypoints

Heuristic detection guesses interior joints (elbow, hip, knee) from the outline. For anatomically exact joints, run a 2D pose estimator (DWPose / anime-pose). Either import its OpenPose/COCO JSON, or deploy the RIGFORGE Pose Space and call it directly above — it rebuilds the rig on the real keypoints.

Rig readiness

Certifies the rig did its basic job: one figure, joints on the body, bilateral symmetry, plausible proportions, and a clean rest-pose bind. This is the self-check the rest of the system builds on.

Recursive foundation

Captures a rig only if it passes readiness, then saves the figure + corrected keypoints as a labeled sample for Alpeccaai-data. Each certified rig becomes training data — the loop that lets her detector improve on her own art.
