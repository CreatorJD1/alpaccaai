⊞ Windows

Her desktop launcher: one thing to run, and she boots with everything she needs.

⇩  Download AlpeccaLauncher.exe

One file, no unzipping — run it and she wakes up.

⇩  Download launcher (zip)
  1. Unzip it inside her folder (next to server.py).
  2. Open the unzipped alpecca-launcher folder.
  3. Double-click run_launcher.bat.

The zip is packed fresh from her source on every download, so it is always current.

🌐 From anywhere

Two ways to reach her beyond this computer, both from her folder.

Same WiFi — opens her to your own network:

python scripts/share.py

Anywhere — a free Cloudflare tunnel (needs cloudflared installed):

python scripts/share.py --tunnel

Privacy: a public tunnel link is UNAUTHENTICATED — anyone with it can chat with her and see her memories. Keep ALPECCA_FILES off while sharing publicly, only hand the link to yourself, and stop it (Ctrl-C) when done. The WiFi option keeps everything on your own network and is the safer choice.
